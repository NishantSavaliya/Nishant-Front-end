Q-12 : WAP to convert years into days and days into years?

Ans :

-> For years into days

            let years = prompt('Enter the number of years')
           
            let day = years * 365;
            console.log(day);

-> For days into years

            let days = prompt('Enter the number of days')
           
            let years = days * 365;
            console.log(years);
